Ex-Video Producer becomes a developer - what gives?

(Honestly, the two industries aren't as far apart as you might think.)


Since being introduced to coding as a child, I had always had an enthusiasm for creating. The rush I got from creating my first Python text game had stayed with me and, as I grew up (and forgot about my childhood interests), led me to a 5-year career as a producer and student filmmaker. I have since realised that my passion for development is irreplaceable.

It all started with Northcoders.

(And Annas.)

2021 had been a difficult year for most of us, myself included. I had lost confidence in my industry, and my soul was craving something different. A growing interest in development and Web 3.0 technology started to consume my spare-time. The stars aligned and, with a little push from a close friend, I left my career and embarked on a journey into the world of coding - starting with a 13-week intensive Northcoders course in software development.
